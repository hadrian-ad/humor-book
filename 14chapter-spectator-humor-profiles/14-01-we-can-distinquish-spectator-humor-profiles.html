Different humor profiles

When put side-by-side, we can often distinguish between an actor acting humor (spectator humor profile) and an actor's own feelings of humor (personal humor profile):

In the images above, we can tell when the actors are pretending humor (spectator humor profile), and when they're feeling humor (personal humor profile).

Consider:

Pretended evaluations are less vividly presented than our actual evaluations—which is true for all our emotions.

In the same way pretended humor is less vivid than actual humor, pretended surprise is less vivid than actual surprise.
