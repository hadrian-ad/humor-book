Chapter 11

Spectator Humor Profile

In this chapter, we’ll look at spectator humor profiles—which is how artists, actors, characters, and so on act or depict humor in spectator entertainment.

Consider:

We said before that each person has a humor profile, which is how we express humor tells, specific to ourselves.

But if we act in a play, we won't transplant our actual humor profile to the stage wholesale to act out humor. Instead, we'll use a spectator humor profile.

We can usually tell the difference between the actor acting humor tells (spectator humor profile) and the actor themselves acting humor (personal humor profile):

Sam and Jennifer are watching a movie where the main character gets ready to sledding with her beau.

As they get dressed to go outside, both romantic leads smile at each other. (←Spectator humor profile)

They reach the top of the hill. When she starts sledding downhill, her face lights up and she laughs as she gets sprayed with powder. It's clear the actor is actually delighted. (←humor profile)

Sam and Jennifer smile. A nice, genuine moment.

Sam asks a stranger to take a picture of him, Jennifer, and Riley. They pose and smile, and then turn to each other and laugh.

As Sam flicks through the pictures later, he first sees their stilted camera smiles. (←spectator humor profile)

Then he sees the pictures of them laughing with each other, faces bright, looking like a family. (←spectator humor profile)

Sam is watching bloopers for Blood Soul Revenge.

Daisy Girl: "Hah!" (←spectator humor profile) *to usurper* (defiant) “You’ll never find Rage Man. He’s a bladed shadow. He’s crimson moonlight. He treads the mmhmm…” *forgets line*

The actor playing the usurper smiles with frustration. The actor playing Daisy Girl laughs. (←humor profiles)

Sam smiles. (←humor profile)

Sam is watching The Trolby Report

Trolby: (in character) "Our enemies say our campaign for city council doesn't have support. Ha! Haha!" (←spectator humor profile) "Well, try saying that to these donors!"

Trolby starts reading off his donor list with intensity. It's just a list of fake names.

The comedian loses his composure and starts laughing. He turns red as he holds paper over his mouth to disguise his laughter. (←humor profile)

Sam smiles. (←Sam enjoys when the actor's evaluations break through)

Sam doesn't have trouble sorting through what's a humor profile, and what's a spectator humor profile.

We'll be talking more about spectator humor profiles throughout this chapter.
