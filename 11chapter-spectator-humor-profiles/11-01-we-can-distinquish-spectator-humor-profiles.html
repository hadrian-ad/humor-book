Pretend evaluations

A spectator humor profile is how characters or other surreal things, like drawings or animations, show humor:

The curtain goes up and Jennifer and Sam walk onto the stage. Jennifer is wearing a garish fish costume, which contrasts with her character's aristocratic demeanor.

Jennifer: *fish head flopping* (aristocratically) "Are you quite sure this plan of yours will work, Kristoff?"

The audience smiles. (←humor profile)

Sam: *looking at the audience* *confidence-man smile* (←spectator humor profile) "The plan is fool proof, Lady Carmine. And..." *swivels to look at Jennifer*

Sam sees Jennifer looking at him earnestly, her fish head bobbing. Sam surpresses a genuine smile. (←humor profile)

Above, the audience and actors can easily distinquish between Sam's acted humor and (spectator humor profile) and Sam's own feeling of humor (humor profile).

Similarly, below Sam can tell when an actor is pretending to smile (spectator humor profile) and when the actor is actually smiling (humor profile).

Sam is watching a movie where the main character goes sledding.

As the romantic lead pushes her down the hill, her face lights up.(←actor has a genuine smile that comes from the actor, not the character)

We don't always have to think about how actors have their own evaluations apart from their characters. In part, that's because movies or shows want to obscure actors' evaluations—and so they'll film or practice enough that the audience won't ever see the actors break character.

But sometimes the contrast between the actor and their character is part of the fun of the entertainment:

Sam is watching a comedian give fake names, but the comedian loses his composure and starts laughing. He turns red as he holds paper over his mouth to disguise his laughter. (←we often track both actors and character emotions during live skits)

Sam is watching bloopers for Blood Soul Revenge.

Daisy Girl: "Hah!" (←spectator humor profile) *to usurper* (defiant) “You’ll never find Rage Man. He’s a bladed shadow. He’s crimson moonlight. He treads the mmhmm…” *forgets line* (←mistake)

Daisy Girl: “Dammit!” (←mistake is larger than OK↔︎mistake-predominant emotions↔︎breaks character)

Sam smiles. (←humor profile)

- OR -

Daisy Girl: *laughs* (←mistake is OK↔︎humor↔︎breaks character↔︎humor profile)

Sam smiles. (←humor profile)
