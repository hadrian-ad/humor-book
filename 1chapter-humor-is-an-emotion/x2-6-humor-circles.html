We'll focus on humor, not genres

We can smile and laugh in any situation, like after:

  • hearing a joke
  • receiving a gift
  • being kissed
  • biking downhill
  • cresting into a beautiful view
  • putting clothes on fresh out of the drier
  • feeling a gust of wind
  • finishing a workout
  • seeing a fox
  • seeing a friend
  • hearing news
  • watching someone get hurt

and so on

Of course, we sometimes smile and laugh at these things—but not always!

To organize our thinking at the beginning, we'll just be focusing on humor:

By focusing on humor, we'll set aside specific situations and instead learn when and why we feel humor, in any case.
