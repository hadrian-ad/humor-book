Overall networks have subnetworks

Since everyone is always radiating emotion tells, we can always make inferences about a room's evaluatory state.

Consider:

If we walk into a room, we can immediately tell if the people there are belligerent, bored, cowering, delighted, focused, posturing, relaxed, scared, studious, worried, and so on—and in what combination.

We can do this even if we don't know the context of those feelings, and even if they're not aware of us!
