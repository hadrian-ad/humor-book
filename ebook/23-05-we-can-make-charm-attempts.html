We can make charm attempts

A humor attempt can either be a comedy attempt (prioritize intensity)—or be a charm attempt (prioritize fittedness):

Jessica: *to a customer* *setting down condiments* “Oh, I love your style!” (←Jessica’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Yasmin: *turns red* *brightens* *beams* “Oh. Thanks!”

Tucker pokes his head into Amber’s office unannounced.

Tucker: “Amber, great work with setting up that meeting today. It was flawless. I don’t know what we’d do without you.” (←Tucker’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Amber: *smiles* “Thank you!”

Riley: *upset* “Mom? I’m sorry. I didn’t mean to break it. Maybe I can make a new one.”

Jennifer: “Aw Riley. It’s okay.” *hugs her* (←Riley’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt) “Let’s just be more careful next time, okay?”

Sam is examining his balding head in the mirror. He rubs it thoughtfully. Jennifer sees him do this, and she grabs him by the arm.

Jennifer: *squeezing him* “You look so handsome.” (←Sam’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Sam gives a releasing series of small laughs, and leans against her affectionately.

Emma: *red-eyed*

Zoe: “What’s wrong, Em?”

Emma: “Nothing. I’ve just realized I’m older now than my brother was when h-he died…”

Zoe: “Aw. Emma.” *hugs* (←Zoe’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Emma: *cries* *laughs*

Trevor: *shopping online* “Should we order it or not?”

Yasmin: “I just can’t decide! I love StormConQuest, but the reviews on the expansion are just so mixed. They say StormConQuest: Seas of Drake and Myrrh really simplifies the resource management. What if that ruins it?”

Trevor: *out of the blue* “What do you think about having a kid?” (←Trevor’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Yasmin: *puts hand on face* *laughs* “Oh god, Trevor. That’s not StormConQuest!”

Trevor: *laughs* “Not now obviously. Just spitballing here, random talk.”

Yasmin’s mother: *sets down a glass of water* “Here, I got you some water!” (←Yasmin’s mother’s humor attempt prioritizes fittedness↔︎charm attempt)

Trevor: *smiles* “Thank you.”

Above, the people making humor attempts were prioritizing fittedness—so they were charm attempts. The intensity of humor didn't have priority of concern.

Restaurantic. Sometimes we think humor is comedy, and so we try to understand all humor in terms of being “comedic.” But that would be odd—a bit like thinking eating is restaurants, and then trying to understand all eating in terms of being “restaurantic.”

That would be using something that came second (comedy/restaurants) to try to explain the much wider thing that came first (humor/eating).
