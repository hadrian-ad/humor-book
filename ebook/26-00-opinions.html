Chapter 26

Opinions

Last chapter, we looked at humor bushes— our humor over time. In this chapter, we'll look at how humor bushes are our opinions.

Our opinion of something is based on our humor bush with it. This is obvious when we think about Emma’s relation to her university:

This means the size of Emma’s humor bush will correspond to her opinion of her university.

This is something we'll be looking at throughout this chapter.

Places and things. At first, it can seem odd to talk about growing a humor bush with something abstract or inanimate—like a university. But it’s not that odd once we think about it.

We also become fond of comedic movies, games, mementos, people, toys, and so on when they generate humor in us—our humor constitutes our good opinion of them. The same is true for broader things, like cities or universities.
