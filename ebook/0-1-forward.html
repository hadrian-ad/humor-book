About Humor in Various Moods in Meter

Welcome to this practical course for serious instruction in humor! Time and time again, it's left readers with the sticky sense that the world is more beautiful and elegant than they had supposed.

Readers often attest to the same pleasing quality of "surprising obviousness" that is present throughout. By re-viewing familiar experiences, and teasing out some shared structure between them, readers are initiated into a useful and profound way of seeing the world.

Both analytical and narrative, this textbook will help you find this abiding sense of practical beauty and usable elegance too.
