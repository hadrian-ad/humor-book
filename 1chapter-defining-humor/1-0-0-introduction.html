1. Defining

Sometimes there's humor, and sometimes there isn't:

Jennifer and Sam take their daughter Riley to a zoo. They see a sign that says GOATS HAVE NO MANNERS.

Jennifer & Sam: *laugh* (←humor)

Riley: "What? What?" (←no humor)

Dave taxis through another country and laughs at a stop sign that's not in English. (← humor)

The taxi driver doesn't laugh and bites back annoyance. (←no humor)

Sometimes we think humor happens at random—but it doesn't. There's a universal pattern to humor. We'll be talking about this pattern throughout this chapter.
