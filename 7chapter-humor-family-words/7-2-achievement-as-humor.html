Achievement as humor

Humor family words

acceptance, accomplishment, achievement , appreciation, belonging, comfort, connection, delight, forgiveness, generosity, insight, joy, realization, reconciliation, release, revelation, relief, safety, success, welcome

Another humor family word is achievement. We feel achievement when we evaluate a mistake as OK:

Lina gets an email that her college decision notification is ready. Her blood freezes.

Heart pounding, she logs on to her account and clicks the ‘view decision update’ letter. Her mouth is dry while it loads.

“Congratulations!” the letter says. Lina leaps up and pumps her fist. “Mom, mom!” she yells, thumping down the stairs. “I got into Pitt!” She jumps up and down and dances, glowing with happiness.

As her mom congratulates her, she puts on a celebratory plastic tiara, feeling invincibly happy. They head out to get pizza, and she catches her reflection in the doors and laughs as she examines herself, and spends the rest of the evening joking with her mom. (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK↔︎humor)

When we feel achievement, we're feeling humor—when we're feeling humor, we feel achievement:

Professor Justin hands back everyone’s literary analysis essay.

Owen: *thinking* “A 'B'? Wow. I totally wrote this literally in the half-hour before it was due.” *laughs* (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK↔︎humor)

Emma: *thinking* "A 'B'? This is the first time I've ever put effort into a literature essay. I feel weirdly proud!" *smiles* (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK↔︎humor)

Zoe: *thinking* "A 'B'?! But I followed the rubric exactly! I had Justin read it early! He should be fired!" (←no achievement↔︎mistake is not OK↔︎no humor)

Trevor is about to ask Yasmin out for the first time. He sweats and his hands shake as he re-reads his text. He feels nauseous. What if she says no? Finally he hits send, and sits on his couch, ashen faced.

Yasmin: *texting* “Sure! I was hoping to hear from you!”

Trevor: *fist pumps* *beams* *laughs* *dances* *takes long ecstatic walk* (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK ↔︎humor)

Emma: *checks phone* “Holy shit. My paper got accepted for that conference!” *laughs* (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK↔︎humor)

Jessica: (excited) “Really?” *laughs* “That’s great, Em! Congratulations!”  (←achievement↔︎mistake is OK↔︎humor)

The word achievement is a word that bundles together humor and that humor's context.
