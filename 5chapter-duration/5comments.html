Sometimes, this process can learn to long durations of humor:

Minh and Trevor check their co-op score. 469.

Trevor: “69. Nice.”

Minh: "Heh" (←Trevor's joke doesn't go against Minh's durable expectations of Trevor's behavior↔︎relatively shorter duration of humor)

- OR -

Minh and his mom check their co-op score. 469.

Mom: "69. Nice."

Minh: (surprised) "Mom!" *laughs* (←Minh's mom's joke goes against Minh's durable expectations of his mother's behavior↔︎longer duration humor)  

Justin: *telegraphing joke* *raises eyebrow* (amused) “… And so then the manager says: two rights don’t make a wrong!”

Heather: *smiles* “Har har.” (←Heather quickly readjusts↔︎shorter humor)

- OR -

Justin: *talking normally* “… and, anyway, then I told Dave right up front that he shouldn’t do that—two rights don’t make a wrong!”

Heather: *laughs* “You mean two wrongs don’t make a right!”

Justin: (perplexed) “That’s what I…” *catches self* “Oh. Oh!” *laughs*

Heather laughs. Justin so rarely makes speaking errors like that. She teases him for a while. (←Heather slowly readjusts↔︎longer humor)
