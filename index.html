This book provides a strong introduction to humor dynamics—an understanding of what humor is, and how it works and plays out in specific and general situations.

We'll then be looking at what all this does to everyone.

Humor dynamics are everywhere. Have you ever shared a glance and smile with a stranger? Laughed after opening a perfect gift? Felt annoyed by the people laughing the table over? Listened to a joke that made the room tense?

These are examples of the sort of dynamics around humor we'll be looking at—something inclusive to laughter, but a bit broader as well.

We'll be going over these humor dynamics from beginning to end—you'll finish substantially better at observing, describing understanding, and producing humor than when you started.

It'll be at least somewhat fun and very interesting—you'll see!
