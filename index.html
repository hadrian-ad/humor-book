Humor in Various Moods and Meters

An Introduction to Humor Dynamics

Welcome!

This book provides an introduction to humor dynamics—thinking about what humor is, how it works, and what it does in specific and general situations.

Essentially, we'll be exploring a mystery: when and why do we smile and laugh?

Yasmin puts on her glasses for the first time. She can see leaves! Clouds have texture! She beams and laughs. (←humor)

Alina is talking on the phone with her younger brother, Artem.

Alina: “I’m sorry things are rough right now. Lots of people have troubles after high school. There’s no shame in being depressed. Listen, I have to go, but I’ll call you soon.”

She hangs up. Artem sits back.

Artem: (thunderstruck) “I have depression!?” (←no humor)*laughs* (←humor) “That makes so much sense. Why didn’t I think of that before?”

How can we understand and think about this smiling and laughing—not just in response to jokes, but all across our lives?

We'll be going over these humor dynamics carefully and in a systematic way. By the time we finish, you'll be better at observing, describing, and producing humor than when you started.
