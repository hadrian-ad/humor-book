Humor in Various Moods and Meters

An Introduction to Humor Dynamics

Welcome!

This book is an introduction to humor dynamics—one of the most important subjects in the world, and also one of the least studied.

For most of us, "humor dynamics" is a new term for familiar experiences:

Zoe is nervous, but Emma cracks a joke. Zoe laughs and becomes more comfortable. (←humor dynamics)

Owen is ruminating, but then his professor compliments his work. He smiles and laughs easily for a while after. (←humor dynamics)

Jessica drops something and laughs. Then, overwhelmed, she starts to cry.

Emma talks with her until Jessica wipes her eyes and gives a small laugh. (←humor dynamics)

Above, the smiles and laughs didn't happen in isolation. Instead, they're part of dynamic experiences.

Consider:

When Emma cracks a joke, the point of interest isn't so much Emma's joke, but Zoe's change from nervous to comfortable. This sort of dynamic is the focus of this book.

In this book, humor refers to smiling and laughing. It's something that happens, or is felt. This isn't static—humor has a "rise and fall" element to it.

To understand this rise and fall, we'll have to think about humor dynamics. That means thinking about changes—about when and why we smile and laugh, and what that does to people and situtations.

In this book, we'll learn to describe and think coherently about these humor dynamics, in ways that give us insight and capability.

A bit like an investigative story, we'll go over the clues from our life piece-by-piece until we assemble a satisfying way of understanding humor and the dynamic it plays in our lives.

Beginnings. Even though this book starts "at the beginning," it's not remedial instruction in humor. Instead, it supplies catalytic insight to our lifetime of observations and experiences with humor—helping set them all into a coherent system.

It's a bit like geometry class. Just because we've spent our life around rectangles and triangles doesn't mean we know everything about them.

In the same way it's useful to understand geometry for some projects, some projects are helped by understand humor dynamics.
