Humor in Various Moods and Meters

An Introduction to Humor Dynamics

Welcome!

In Humor in Various Moods and Meter, you'll learn when and why we smile or laugh—and much more besides.

Consider:

This is an introduction to humor dynamics. If we throw a rock into water, something happens—not just the rock, but the ripples. This book is interested in these "ripples" caused by humor.

By the end, you'll have become significantly better at observing, describing, understanding, and producing humor than you were before.
