Humor in Various Moods and Meters

An Introduction to Humor Dynamics

Welcome!

This book provides an introduction to humor dynamics—thinking about what humor is, how it works, and what it does in specific and general situations.

We'll learn when and why we smile or laugh, and then be looking at what all this does to everyone and the overall situation.

We'll be going over these humor dynamics carefully and in a systematic way. By the time we finish, you'll be better at observing, describing understanding, and producing humor than when you started.
