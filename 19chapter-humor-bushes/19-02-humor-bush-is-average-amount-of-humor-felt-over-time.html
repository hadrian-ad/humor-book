We can average humor over time by topic

Humor bushes are associtated with specific topics.

For instance, when Zoe’s topic ‘taking the bus’ is active, the topic tracks the average humor felt over time:

Zoe has a humor bush with taking the bus—which isn't related to all the humor Zoe has felt, about everything. It's just all the humor she's felt about taking the bus.

This is useful when we want to think about humor over various timescales—so not just the average humor felt over two minutes as she boarded the bus, but also the average humor she's felt over several years of riding the busses near campus.

Consider:

We can set different time or topic bounds for humor bushes. We can look at Zoe's humor from a single bus route or from all bus routes—or from this year, or the last ten years.
