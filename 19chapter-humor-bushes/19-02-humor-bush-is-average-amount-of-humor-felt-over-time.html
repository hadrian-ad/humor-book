Humor bush is average amount of humor felt over time

A humor bushes can track the cumulative amount of humor we feel generally, but we can also think of the cumulative amount of humor we have specifically—we can think of individual topics as having a humor bush too.

Humor bushes are useful when we want to think about humor over a various time scales—so not just the average humor Zoe feels over two minutes as she boards the bus, but also the average humor she's felt over several years of riding the busses near campus.

We can segment humor bushes by smaller topics too—like by breaking down 'taking the bus' into 'taking route 11' and 'taking route 47.' These smaller topics will have their own humor bushes.

If route 11 is always crowded and she has to stand, while she gets to sit with Emma on route 47, 'taking route 47' will have a larger humor bush than 'taking route 11.'
